# BatchGen Text with AI

**BatchGen Text with AI** is a Windows desktop app for generating batches of written content using AI — blog posts, LinkedIn posts, tweets, short stories, technical docs, and more.

You bring your own AI API key (BYOK). The app connects directly to the provider of your choice — no middleman, no subscription, no data stored anywhere except your own machine.

---

## How it works

1. **Configure your persona** — define the voice, tone, and style the AI will write in
2. **Load writing samples** _(optional)_ — the app analyzes your samples and mimics the writing style
3. **Pick your content list** — a simple CSV or YAML file with titles and optional notes
4. **Generate** — the app sends each item through the AI and saves the output to a timestamped folder

A batch of 20 articles takes a few minutes. Output lands in your Documents folder, ready to use.

---

## Key features

- **9 AI provider presets** — OpenAI, DeepSeek, Groq, Mistral, OpenRouter, Together AI, xAI, Fireworks, Cerebras, or any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint
- **7 content templates** — Blog Article, LinkedIn Post, X/Twitter Post (with thread mode), Facebook Post, Short Story, Technical Document, and a fully editable Custom template
- **Writing Style DNA** — paste in a few of your own articles and the AI reverse-engineers your voice, sentence patterns, and vocabulary
- **Quote injection** — load a YAML quote bank; relevant quotes are woven in naturally per article
- **Multi-language output** — generate content in 13 European languages
- **Secure API key storage** — keys are encrypted on-device using Windows DPAPI, never transmitted anywhere except directly to your chosen AI provider
